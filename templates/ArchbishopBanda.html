THE MOST REVEREND DR. ALICK BANDA ARCHBISHOP OF LUSAKA

Born on 15th November 1963 in Mufulira. He was ordained Priest on 7th August 1994 in Ndola. Post-graduate studies and doctorate in Church-State Law (Canon Law), in Germany. Personal and confidential Secretary to the late Bishop Dennis H. de Jong. Parish Priest, Sts. Peter and Paul, Chingola, Chancellor of the Catholic Diocese of Ndola. Consecrated as the third Bishop of Solwezi, on 29th July 2007. Appointed Coadjutor Bishop of Ndola Diocese on 13th November, 2009. Succeeded Bishop Noel O’Reagan on 16th January,2010. Installed Bishop of Ndolaon 13th February 2010. Appointed Archbishop and Apostolic Administrator of Ndola Diocese on 30th January, 2018. Installed Archbishop of Lusaka: 14th April 2018
