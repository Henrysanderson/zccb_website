THE RIGHT REV. EVANS CHINYAMA CHINYEMBA, OMI BISHOP OF MONGU

Born on August, 1967, in Lukulu, Western Province of Zambia. He hails from Sancta Maria Oblate Mission in Lukulu. Evan Chinyama Chinyemba entered the Congregation of Oblates of Mary Immaculate pre- novitiate formation program in Lusaka, in January, 1991. On August 19th, 2000 he was ordained to the Priesthood at Sancta Maria Catholic Church in Lukulu, Western Province. On February, 15, 2011 he was appointed Bishop of Mongu Diocese. His Episcopal Ordination as second Bishop of Mongu Diocese was on 28th May, 2011 at Our Lady of Lourdes Cathedral in Mongu.
