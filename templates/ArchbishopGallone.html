ARCHBISHOP GIANFRANCO GALLONE

Archbishop Gianfranco Gallone was born on 20th April, 1960 in Ceglie Messapica in the diocese of Oria in Italy. He was ordained priest for the diocese of Oria on 3rd September, 1988.
He is ordained Bishop San Pietro, Basilica, Roma, Diocese of Rome, Italy. On 5th Febraury,2019 was appointed Apostolic Nuncio to Zambia and Titular Archbishop of Motula on 2dn February and ordained on 8th May,2019. Archbishop Gallone entered the Diplomatic Service of the Holy See on 19th June 2000. Subsequently he was posted to Mozambique, Israel, Slovakia, India and Sweden.
